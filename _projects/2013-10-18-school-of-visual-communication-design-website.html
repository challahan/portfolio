In Spring 2013, I started work with a student design class on campus (Glyphix) to development an updated website for the School of Visual Communication Design.

My role in this project:

  • Worked with designers to approve wireframe comp designs, based on Kent State's marketing standards and content management system
  • Translated design comps into standards-compliant CSS and HTML
  • Integrated designs into Kent State's content management system
  • Developed custom functionality for Student Work gallery and faculty/staff profiles
  • Provided support for the department as they developed pages and content
