Glyphix is a student-staffed design studio within the School of Visual Communication Design at Kent State University.  I worked with the Glyphix class to turn their design concepts and wireframes into an HTML/CSS template, built into university's content management system.   The site launched in January 2014.

My role in this project:

  • Used SASS/Bourbon to style the website, based on annotated Photoshop mockups provided to me by the class
  • Set up the templates in CommonSpot, the university's content management system
  • Integrated the photo galleries with Flickr, using the Galleria plugin
  • Guided the class through the process of content entry and architecture in the content management system
